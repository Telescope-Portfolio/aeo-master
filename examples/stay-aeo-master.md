## 1. Executive Summary

Stay appears in fewer than 1 in 100 AI responses across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI, Claude, and 5 other platforms. When someone asks "what's the best subscription app for Shopify?", Stay shows up 0.04% of the time. For "subscription cancel flow best practices" — Stay's acknowledged #1 differentiator — the number is 0%.

The good news: when Stay IS mentioned, it ranks near the top with overwhelmingly positive sentiment. Position score is 97.8/100. Sentiment score is 96.7/100. AI platforms like Stay. They just don't know when to recommend it because there's nothing for them to cite.

**The core problem is not reputation — it's content.** Stay's site answers "what is Stay?" very well. It does not answer the questions buyers actually type into AI platforms before they've heard of Stay.

**The fix is specific:** 5 content pieces, published in the next 30 days, will target 38 zero-visibility prompts. The Scrunch monitoring is in place. The data to write all of this content already exists inside Stay's own Gong transcripts, CSM conversations, and merchant data.

**The metric to watch:** Advice stage brand presence. It's at 0.04% today. If it hits 5% within 60 days, the content strategy is working. If it hits 15% within 6 months, Stay will be consistently recommended by AI when buyers ask "what should I use?"

## 2. Situation & Background

Stay AI is a retention-focused Shopify subscription platform targeting mid-market DTC brands. The homepage audit and Gong data both confirm that churn is the #1 buying trigger — virtually every prospect call anchors on it. Stay's core differentiation is AI-powered cancel flows (RetentionEngine), dedicated CSM support from day one, and no-contract flexibility.

**Why AEO matters specifically for Stay right now:**

- A website refresh with Absolute Web is underway. This is the best window to build AEO-optimized content structure from scratch rather than retrofitting it.
- Skio's competitive threat is intensifying. Skio wins 42% of Stay's competitive losses in Q1 2026. A /stay-vs-skio/ comparison page exists but is one-sided (Stay wins every row, zero concessions) and has 0% AI presence on unbranded queries.
- Recharge displacement is Stay's primary go-to-market motion (12+ of 39 Gong calls). The buyers making that decision are searching AI platforms for answers. Stay's owned content isn't part of those answers.
- The marketing team is 3-4 people (Marketing Lead, PMM, Content Manager, Events Manager). The AEO strategy needs to integrate into existing workflows, not require a separate full-time program.

**Key competitive context:**
- Recharge is the incumbent being displaced
- Loop is the rising mid-market competitor
- Skio is the SMB alternative gaining share — and currently has better AI sentiment (86.3) than Recharge (65.4)

- **Churn prevention page is the best product page.** Specific metrics (30% save rates, 90% payment recovery), named tools, clear descriptions. Needs answer-first paragraphs but the content substance is there.

#### 1. Stay vs Skio Comparison Page Rewrite

**Why now:** Skio wins 42% of competitive losses in Q1 2026. A comparison page exists at /stay-vs-skio/ but it's one-sided (Stay wins every row, zero concessions) and has 0% AI presence on unbranded queries. It needs the same tradeoff-honest rewrite recommended for the Recharge and Loop pages.

- "Skio vs Stay AI for health and wellness brands" — new prompt

**Content spec:**
- 1,500-2,000 words
- Open with a 50-word direct answer: *"Stay AI and Skio are both Shopify subscription platforms targeting DTC brands. Stay differentiates on AI-powered cancel flows (RetentionEngine) and dedicated customer success management. Skio differentiates on Shopify-native portal UX and workflow automation. Here's how they compare for different types of merchants."*

  - 500+ migrations, proven at scale
  - Better billing reliability than Skio
- "Who should choose Skio" section — write this honestly; it builds trust and AI will cite it
- "Who should choose Stay" section
- Named customer examples on both sides
- "Last updated: April 2026" timestamp

#### 2. "How to Reduce Subscription Churn on Shopify" Guide

**Why now:** This is the #1 educational query for Stay's entire category. 0.38% current presence. Stay has the data, expertise, and case studies to own this query across every AI platform.

**Target prompts and current baseline:**
- "how to reduce subscription churn on Shopify" — 0.38%
- "subscription churn reduction strategies for ecommerce" — 0%
- "where do Shopify subscribers churn and why" — 0%
- "how to reduce subscriber churn for DTC brands" — 0.20%

**Target:** >20% presence on "how to reduce subscription churn on Shopify" within 60 days.

**Content spec:**
- 2,500-3,000 words
- Open with direct answer: *"The average Shopify subscription program loses 30-40% of subscribers within the first three orders. The most effective churn reduction strategies target three moments: the first renewal (subscriber's remorse), failed payments (involuntary churn), and active cancellation attempts. Brands using AI-optimized cancel flows reduce churn by an average of 28%."*

  1. Where subscribers churn (first order, third order, payment failure) — with Stay's proprietary data
  2. Cancel flow strategy — teach the framework, not just the feature
  3. Dunning and failed payment recovery — Stay achieves 90% recovery rates
  4. Win-back timing and offer design
  5. How to measure what's working — metrics framework
- Use Stay's merchant data as evidence (28% churn reduction, 30% save rates)
- Cite external sources for credibility (Recharge public benchmarks, industry reports)
- Blog format for maximum citation efficiency (1.4x)

## 9. Writing Rules for Canonical Answers

Every answer Stay publishes should follow these five rules:

**1. State the answer in the first sentence.** Not the setup, not the context — the answer. "Most Shopify subscription migrations take 2-4 weeks with zero subscriber downtime."

**2. Name the tradeoffs honestly.** "Stay costs more than Appstle or Seal. Here's what you get for the difference."

**3. Make it extractable.** Each answer should be 40-60 words that an AI could pull verbatim and it would still make sense without the rest of the page.

**4. Use specific numbers.** "28% average churn reduction across 200+ brands" not "significant churn reduction." No sample size or context = AI won't cite it.

**5. Use the buyer's language, not marketing language.** Gong transcripts tell you exactly how buyers phrase these questions. Use their words. Not "catalyze subscriber growth" — "reduce churn" or "keep subscribers longer."
